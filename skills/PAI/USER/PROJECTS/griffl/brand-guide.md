# Brand Rules: griffl.org

## Voice & Tone
- Reflective and practice-oriented with clear explanations
- Thoughtful analysis of instructional design and solopreneurship
- Occasional humor when it lightens complex topics
- Personal insights balanced with professional expertise
- Encouraging but realistic about challenges

## Target Audience
- Instructional designers and trainers in DACH region
- Solopreneurs building educational businesses
- Learning professionals seeking practical insights
- German-speaking professionals in education/training

## Content Themes
- Instructional design methodologies and best practices
- Solopreneur challenges and solutions
- Learning technology and tools
- Personal productivity and business building
- Cross-cultural perspectives on education

## Formatting Style
- Use clear headings (H2, H3) for structure
- Include bullet points and practical checklists
- Highlight actionable advice in callout boxes
- Add relevant examples from personal experience
- End with clear takeaways or next steps

## SEO Considerations
- Target German and English keywords
- Focus on "how-to" and problem-solving content
- Include local/regional context when relevant
- Optimize for professionals searching for practical solutions

## Content Length
- Blog posts: 1500-2500 words
- LinkedIn posts: 3-5 paragraphs with clear hook
- Newsletter: 500-800 words with 2-3 key insights
